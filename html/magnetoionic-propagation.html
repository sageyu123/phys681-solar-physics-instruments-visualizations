Magnetoionic Waves

Electromagnetic modes in a cold magnetized plasma (solar corona). Only the x- and o-mode escape to the observer; z- and whistler modes sit inside stopbands — frequency windows where n2 < 0, so the wave is evanescent and cannot propagate.

Refractive Index n2(ω)
Shaded bands = stopbands where n2 < 0. A wave generated inside a stopband is evanescent — it decays exponentially and cannot escape. This is why z-mode (between ωL and ωUH) and whistler (ω < ωce) waves stay trapped.
