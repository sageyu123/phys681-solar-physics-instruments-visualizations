Optical Depth Sets the First Read of a Radio Spectrum

This explorer keeps the physics deliberately simple: one emitting slab, one background, and a free-free opacity that falls toward high frequency. Move the controls until the same source changes from optically thick to optically thin, then watch how the spectrum, the line-of-sight cartoon, and the diagnostic summary update together.
